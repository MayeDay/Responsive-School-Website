About Us

Welcome to Choosen University, your number one source for all things education. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of educational tools, with an emphasis on honesty, integrity, values.

Founded in 1996 by Edward James VI, Choosen University has come a long way from its beginnings in New Count, West Virginia. When Edward James VI first started out, their passion for education drove them to start this school.
